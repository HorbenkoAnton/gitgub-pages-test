Xenn is a brand that combines crochet with psychology and art. It specializes in creating clothes, accessories, and plush figures where form meets meaning. Each project is designed with emotions, relationships, and symbolism in mind — it’s not just a product, but a carrier of ideas. The brand was born from the need to express personal experiences through craftsmanship. Its main technique is crochet, treated as a tool for storytelling and for addressing meaningful topics. Xenn’s designs often explore themes related to psychology, transformation, and inner growth. Xenn is aimed at people who seek more than just aesthetics — those who want to understand the context, feel the emotion, and find deeper value in fashion.
